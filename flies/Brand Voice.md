# Brand Voice & Visual Identity

## Palette
| Name | Hex |
|---|---|
| Ink | `#1A1A18` |
| Mocha | `#8B7355` |
| Cream | `#FAF8F3` |

## Fonts
- Headings: Cambria
- Body: Calibri

## Document tone
- Minimal prose.
- Banking/fintech terminology, used correctly, not decoratively.
- Tables and visuals over paragraphs.
- No filler, no hedging language, no marketing fluff.

## Applies to
Deck, scope docs, build guide, [[Technical Architecture#EOD reconciliation flow (locked)|EOD Recon Report template]], any external-facing PDF/DOCX/PPTX deliverable. Does not govern chat/conversation style.
